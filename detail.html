Cold Chicken with Paprika Mayonnaise

January 1, 2014 by MARION MANSFIELD

Serves: 4

Cooking time: 2 hours

  • 1 boiling chicken
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 sticks celery, chopped
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 1 bay lead
  • salt, pepper

Paprika Mayonnaise:

  • 2 eggs yolks
  • 1 cup (250ml) oil
  • 1 tablespoon French mustard
  • 1 teaspoon tomato paste
  • 1 crisp lettuce

Place chicekn in a saucepan with vegetables, bayleaf, sald and pepper. Barely cover with water, bring to boil, cover saucepan and simmer for 1:30-2 hours.Remove chicken from saucepan and allow to cool.Strain chicken stockm refrigerate, and remove fat. Serve consomme as first course.

To make mayonnaise, drip oil onto egg yolks, stirring constantly, or blend in electric blender.When consistency is right, add paprika, mustard and tomato paste.Season to taste. Arrange crisp lettuce leaves on a serving plate, place chicken on bed of lettuce and spoonover mayoonaise. Serve with ratatouille and crusty bread.s

Easy Mushroom Curry Over Rice

January 1, 2014 by MARION MANSFIELD

Serves: 3

Cooking time: 15 Min

Ingredients:

  • 3 button mushrooms, sliced
  • 3 fresh shiitake mushrooms, remove the ends and finely sliced
  • 1 small box of beach mushroom (I use brown and white mix), remove the hard ends
  • 1/2 red onion, chopped
  • 1 tbsp. vegetable cooking oil
  • 100g golden curry paste, can be purchased in Asian stores or on amazon
  • 2 eggs for serving, whisked (optional)
  • 3 servings rice
  • Spring onion for decoration

Steps:

1. Prepare all of the ingredients.

2. Heat oil over pan and fry red onion until aromatic and slightly seared. Place the mushroom in and fry for 1 or 2 minutes until well softened.

3. Add curry paste and 3 cups of water. Simmer to boil and heat for another minute until well thickened.

4. Pan-fry the egg and top this vegan curry with steamed rice or noodles.
